What We Offer

We have a knack for identifying suitable land with potential, we then secure planning to build wonderful homes. We strive to deliver a service which not only provides more housing but also transform land to fit the surrounding area and turn it into a wonderful space.

Before

Cromwell Road project, a semi-detatched house we spotted with great potential for growth. We secured the property and worked on planning for a side, rear and loft extensions. The house was completely gutted and rebuilt from the bottom up.

After

We completely transformed this end of terrance house which is now barely recognisable. We installed three large flat skylights in the six meter rear extension, which were complimented by a five meter aluminium bifolding doors. This brought in that much needed light and view into the property. The living room now opens up into a large open-space kitchen/diner.
